https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/windstream-2012-sarmento-vineyard-pinot-noir-central-coast-santa-lucia-highlands/	Windstream 2012 Sarmento Vineyard Pinot Noir (Santa Lucia Highlands)	91	While this bottling is always interesting, it is dependent on vintage conditions. This bottling, from the excellent 2012 harvest, is Windstream's best ever, a provocative example of a cool-climate Pinot from a fine vineyard in an excellent year. It's bone dry, tart in acidity and hugely complex, offering layers of raspberry sour candy, cherry-pie filling, persimmons, tobacco spices and just a touch of smoky oak. The mouthfeel is silky and delightful. As good as this wine is, give it at least until 2018 to develop further.
