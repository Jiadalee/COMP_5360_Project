https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/delaire-2003-vineyard-in-sky-red-stellenbosch/	Delaire 2003 Vineyard in the Sky Red (Stellenbosch)	92	Dark, yet with intense red color, this is a smooth, chocolatey, sexy wine, great for a steak but delectable on its own. Dark, meaty aromas incorporate casses and cedar, while the palate is even blacker, with charred meat, black fruits and forest floor, with blueberry and cedar following. A bruiser, with a big feel and big tannins to match, the finish is a bit shut down, so buy a case and try a bottle over the next dozen years.
