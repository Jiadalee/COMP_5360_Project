https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/hartford-court-2007-far-coast-pinot-noir-sonoma-coast/	Hartford Court 2007 Far Coast Pinot Noir (Sonoma Coast)	93	From the winery's Annapolis Vineyard, at the cool, northern tip of the “true” Sonoma Coast, comes this chunky young Pinot Noir.
