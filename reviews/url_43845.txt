It's the kind of Chard that's sometimes called Chablisian because of the firm minerality and streamlined, tightly metallic quality. The '04 is complex and layered, offering fruit pie filling flavors of kiwis, limes and guavas with the caramel and vanilla of well-charred new oak. It's one of the few Chardonnays that will benefit from midterm aging. Drink now—2010.
