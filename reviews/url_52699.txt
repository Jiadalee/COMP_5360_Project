https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/moss-wood-2003-amys-blend-cabernet-sauvignon-margaret-river/	Moss Wood 2003 The Amy's Blend Cabernet Sauvignon (Margaret River)	89	The “blend” in the wine's name refers to the fact that the grapes come from two vineyards: Glenmore (66%) and Montgomery Brothers (34%).
