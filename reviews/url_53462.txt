https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/darenberg-2003-laughing-magpie-shiraz-viognier-red-mclaren-vale/	D'Arenberg 2003 The Laughing Magpie Shiraz Viognier Red (McLaren Vale)	88	Meaty, peppery aromas give way to ones of cherry pie filling after airing. Rhone-styled, perhaps, on the palate, with a cracked-pepper quality accenting the black fruit; there's a lifted quality—the Viognier's floral notes—to the black cherry fruit, that feels overdone. This is a tasty wine, to be sure, but one whose 6% Viognier is about 3% too much.
