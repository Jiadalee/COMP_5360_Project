https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/brumby-canyon-2002-jillaroo-red-south/	Brumby Canyon 2002 Jillaroo Red Red (South Australia)	87	The name requires another Australian-to-American English translation: A "brumby" is a wild Australian horse; a "jillaroo," a farm worker. Thankfully, this wine doesn't taste like anything you'd find in a stable: it offers very ripe black cherry flavors, with some black soil joining in on the finish. Beaujolais-sized in body, this Shriraz (60%), Grenache (33%), Mataro (7%) blend is an inexpensive, easy quaffer.
