https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/torres-1998-grans-muralles-red-conca-barbera/	Torres 1998 Grans Muralles Red (Conca de Barberà)	87	The wine sounds pretty impressive: It's comprised of local Catalan grape varities Monastrell, Garnacha Tinta, Garró, Samsó and Cariñena, which are all grown at Grans Muralles, a single vineyard that dates back to the Middle Ages. And very good it is, though it doesn't taste as singular as its heritage suggests. Black plum and cherry notes are couched in brambly, oak flavors. The wine has woodsy, rustic tannins in the mouth, and even more on the finish. —D.M.
