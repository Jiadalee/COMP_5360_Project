https://www.winemag.com/buying-guide/pio-cesare-1996-il-bricco-nebbiolo-barbaresco/	Pio Cesare 1996 Il Bricco  (Barbaresco)	91	This Barbaresco is big and maybe a touch “international” for some purists, but it maintains a sense of place through its classic structure.
